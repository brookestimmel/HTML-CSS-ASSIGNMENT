About

This is an easy recipe to make chocolate chip cookies with only five ingredients and three steps.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Sift flour and brown sugar into a bowl; stir in eggs. Mix butter into flour mixture until dough is creamy; fold in chocolate chips. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 3 inches apart.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until slightly brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Cool.
